Practice Areas

Fintech & Financial Services Transactions

✦

We advise fintech companies, investors, and financial institutions on regulated financial services transactions in emerging markets.

  • Fintech structuring and regulatory strategy
  • Licensing and engagement with financial regulators (e.g., CBN, SEC)
  • Venture capital, growth-stage, and strategic fundraising
  • M&A, strategic investments, and partnerships
  • Payments, digital banking, lending, and financial infrastructure arrangements

We help founders and investors navigate regulation without slowing growth.

Energy, Oil & Gas & Energy Transition

✦

We advise international and local sponsors on energy and natural resources transactions, with a strong focus on regulatory alignment and execution.

  • Upstream acquisitions, divestments, and asset transfers
  • Farm-in and farm-out transactions
  • Gas processing, midstream, and energy infrastructure JVs
  • Gas-to-power and energy transition projects
  • Operator, investor, and lender documentation

We support transactions from conception through regulatory approvals and financial close.

Real Estate & Infrastructure Transactions

✦

We act as transaction counsel on capital deployment into real assets and infrastructure.

  • Real estate development structuring and SPVs
  • Project finance and construction-linked financing
  • Private debt and equity raises
  • Infrastructure concessions, PPPs, and structured investments
  • Investor, sponsor, and contractor documentation

We structure transactions to meet both investor protection standards and local execution realities.

Cross-Border & Private Capital Structuring
